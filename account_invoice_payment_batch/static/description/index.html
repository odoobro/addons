Account Invoice Payment Batch

Pay many Customer Invoices / Vendor Bills at the same time and take into account the advance payments.

In Odoo 10, You can make payment for many Customer invoices / Vendor Bills at the same time, but it's not take into account the advance payment. This module give you guys 2 menus:
1. Invoicing > Sale > Invoice Payment Batch: Pay for Customer Invoice
2. Invoicing > Purchase > Bill Payment Batch: Pay for Vendor Bill

Customer Invoices

 

If `Payment Difference` is larger than 0.0, an Advance payment will be recorded.
